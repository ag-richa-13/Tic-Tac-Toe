# Tic-Tac-Toe Game

A simple Tic-Tac-Toe game built using Unity. The game features three exciting gameplay modes for a variety of experiences:

- **Play With AI**: Challenge the computer in a single-player mode.
- **Play With Offline Friend**: Play with a friend on the same device in local multiplayer mode.
- **Play With Online Friend**: Play with your friends online via multiplayer mode.

## Features

- **Intelligent AI**: Compete against a smart AI that adjusts its difficulty based on your level.
- **Local Multiplayer**: Play with your friends on the same device with easy-to-understand controls.
- **Online Multiplayer**: Challenge your friends online, with real-time gameplay and matchmaking.
- **User-friendly Interface**: A clean and simple UI that is easy to navigate for players of all ages.
- **Cross-platform Compatibility**: Play across different platforms supported by Unity (to be specified later).
